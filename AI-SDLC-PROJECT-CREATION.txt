AI-SDLC LESSONS LEARNED
=======================

Successful AI Integrations:
1. Code Generation: AI excels at boilerplate generation and pattern recognition
2. Documentation: AI significantly improves documentation quality and consistency
3. Testing: AI-assisted test generation provides comprehensive coverage
4. Educational Content: AI helps structure optimal learning progressions

Areas Requiring Human Expertise:
1. Educational Pedagogy: Human insight essential for learning objective design
2. Creative Design: AI assists but human creativity drives innovative educational features
3. Complex Business Logic: Human expertise needed for sophisticated simulation engines
4. User Experience Design: AI informs but human empathy guides educational UX decisions

Balanced AI-Human Collaboration:
- AI handles repetitive, pattern-based tasks (40% of development work)
- Humans focus on creative, strategic, and domain-specific decisions (60% of development work)
- Continuous feedback loop between AI suggestions and human validation
- AI amplifies human capabilities rather than replacing human expertise
